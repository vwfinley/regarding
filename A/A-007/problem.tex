\section{Problem}
\paragraph{Given:}
\begin{itemize}
\item Two overlapping circles of equal radius.
\end{itemize}

\paragraph{Find:}
\begin{enumerate}
\item An alternate method to represent and construct tangent ogives.
\item Relationship between tangent ogives and circle intersections.
\item Formula to calculate tangent ogive cross-sectional areas.
\item Forumula to calculate volume of the tangent ogive.
\item Area of circle: intersections, unions and differences.
\item Volume of revolved circle intersections.
\end{enumerate}
